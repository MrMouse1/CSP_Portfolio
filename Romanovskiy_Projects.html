    Project: AOI Circuit Design - Sentry Turret

    Course: DE (Digital Electronics)
    Duration: 3 weeks
    Objective: Create a circuit for a real-life application

    My team and I have explored different topics for this project until we stopped at the military protection of countries located near and within dangerous regions. Our design statement was to "create a defense system that can detect the difference between an enemy or anything else that may be moving." What stood out in this project in comparison to others we have done before is step-by-step cooperation, where not only the parts of the whole divided between team members but those segments follow each other one by one within the time limits.

    This project teached me not only to take care of my part of the job, but also to pay attention to others; to make sure the team is working like clockwork.
